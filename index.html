About Us

Welcome to ARK KIDOID, a hub of creativity, learning, and innovation for young minds

At ARK KIDOID, we believe that every child is unique, with talents waiting to be discovered and nurtured. Our mission is to empower children through holistic education programs that inspire curiosity, enhance skills, and ignite a lifelong love for learning

We offer a diverse range of workshops and classes designed to cater to various interests and foster all-round development

Robotics

Unleash your child's inner innovator with our robotics program. Through hands-on activities, kids learn about coding, engineering, and problem-solving while building and programming robots to tackle exciting challenges

Chess

Our chess program sharpens strategic thinking, improves concentration, and teaches the art of planning ahead. Whether your child is a beginner or an advanced player, we help them master the game while boosting their confidence

Abacus

Our abacus program enhances mathematical skills, mental calculations, and memory power. It's a fun and interactive way for kids to develop a strong foundation in numbers and logic.

Public Speaking

We help children find their voice and express themselves confidently. Our public speaking workshops focus on communication skills, storytelling, and stage presence, preparing them to shine in any setting

Dance

From classical to contemporary, our dance classes offer an energetic way for kids to stay active, learn rhythm, and express themselves through movement and music

Why Choose Us?

• Expert Trainers: Our team consists of passionate professionals who bring expertise and enthusiasm to every class.

• Interactive Learning: We believe in hands-on, engaging methods that make learning fun and impactful

•Holistic Development: Ourprograms aim to nurture cognitive, emotional, and physical growth.

• Safe & Supportive Environment: We ensure a safe, inclusive, and encouraging space where children thrive.

Join us and embark on an exciting journey of growth, creativity, and discovery.
